How to Become a Tutor

Create Your Account

Start your journey by creating an account.

Enter your email to start. If you have an account, log in. If not, you'll be guided to register.

Profile Setup

Set up your profile with all the necessary details.

Fill in your personal information, upload a profile picture, and write a brief bio about yourself. This helps students get to know you better.

Tutor Details

Provide your tutoring details and preferences.

Specify the subjects you want to teach, your availability, and your preferred teaching methods. You can also set your hourly rate here.

Verification

Submit a short clip of yourself explaining who you are and your experience.

This video helps us verify your identity and qualifications. Make sure to highlight your teaching experience and any relevant certifications.

Review & Submit

Review your information and submit your application.

Double-check all the details you have provided. Once you are satisfied, submit your application. Our team will review it and get back to you within a few days.

Common Questions

What qualifications do I need to become a tutor?

We welcome tech professionals, educators, and self-taught experts. You should have a strong understanding of the subjects you wish to teach and a passion for helping others learn.

How do I set my rates?

You can set your rates starting as low as $20 per hour. You have the flexibility to adjust your rates based on your experience and the demand for your expertise.

Can I teach both online and in-person?

Yes, you can offer lessons online, in-person, or both. You have the flexibility to choose the mode of teaching that suits you best.

How do I get paid?

Payments are processed through our secure platform. You will receive payments directly to your bank account or preferred payment method.

What support do I get as a tutor?

As a member of the Tech Tutor Hub community, you'll have access to exclusive resources, networking opportunities, and support from our dedicated team to help you succeed.
